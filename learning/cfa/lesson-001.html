🎓 道德与职业准则 — 导论

Ethics and Professional Standards — Introduction · 30 Minutes

🎯 学习目标

  • 理解 CFA 道德准则的重要性及考试权重
  • 掌握道德准则的六大组成部分
  • 识别并避免利益冲突、抢跑交易和内幕交易

一、什么是 CFA 道德准则？

CFA 协会制定了《道德与职业行为准则》，这是 CFA 考试中最重要也最容易丢分的部分。

  • 权重最高（15-20%）= 约 36-48 道题
  • 道德调节机制：如果道德部分表现差，即使总分达标也可能不及格
  • 情景题为主：考题都是情景题，不是死记硬背

💡 记忆技巧： 把这六条想象成"一个金融从业者的做人底线"——对客户的底线、对自己的底线、对市场的底线。

道德准则的六大组成部分

序号组成部分核心理解
1以诚信、胜任、勤勉、尊重和道德方式行事你的行为 = 行业声誉
2将投资行业的诚信和客户利益置于自身利益之上客户永远第一
3运用合理判断并保持独立的专业判断独立思考，不盲从
4自己践行并鼓励他人以专业和道德方式行事以身作则
5维护全球资本市场的诚信和活力不操纵市场
6保持并提升专业能力永不停止学习

二、案例解析

案例 1：抢跑交易 (Front Running)

情景：张分析师准备向基金客户推荐某股票，他先用个人账户买入。

❌ 违规： 客户订单必须优先于个人交易。这是严重违规。

案例 2：重大非公开信息 (MNPI)

情景：李分析师在会议上无意听到 CEO 透露业绩超预期。

❌ 违规： 即使是无意听到的，属于内幕信息，不得使用。

三、自测练习

Q1. 王经理给养老基金客户推荐产品，应推荐？
✅ B. 最适合客户风险偏好的产品

Q2. 赵分析师发现朋友持有他推荐的股票，应？
✅ C. 主动披露利益冲突

Q3. 以下哪个违反职业道德准则？
✅ C. 向客户保证"这只股票一定涨"

📌 今日总结

  • 客户利益优先是核心
  • 内幕交易 / 前置交易 / 保证收益 = 三条红线
  • 道德模块权重最高，需要场景判断力

🎯 Learning Objectives

  • Understand the importance and weight of the CFA Code of Ethics.
  • Master the six components of the Code of Ethics.
  • Identify and avoid conflicts of interest, front running, and insider trading.

1. What is the CFA Code of Ethics?

The CFA Institute has established a Code of Ethics and Standards of Professional Conduct, which is the most important and most frequently tested section of the exam.

  • Highest weight (15-20%) = approx. 36-48 questions.
  • Ethics "bump" rule: Weak ethics performance may lead to failure even with a passing total score.
  • Scenario-based: Questions test practical judgment, not rote memorization.

💡 Memory Tip: Think of these six components as the "professional bottom line" — your baseline, the client's baseline, and the market's baseline.

The Six Components

#ComponentKey Idea
1Act with integrity, competence, diligence, respect, and in an ethical mannerYour behavior reflects on the profession
2Place the integrity of the investment profession and clients' interests above your ownClients come first, always
3Use reasonable care and exercise independent professional judgmentThink independently; don't follow the crowd
4Practice and encourage others to practice ethicallyLead by example
5Promote the integrity and viability of global capital marketsDon't manipulate markets
6Maintain and improve professional competenceNever stop learning

2. Example Questions

Example 1: Front Running

Scenario: Analyst Zhang buys a stock for his personal account before recommending it to fund clients.

❌ Violation: Client orders must take priority over personal trades. This is a serious violation.

Example 2: Material Nonpublic Information (MNPI)

Scenario: Analyst Li overhears a CEO revealing earnings data at a conference.

❌ Violation: Even if overheard unintentionally, it is MNPI and cannot be used for trading.

3. Self-Test

Q1. Manager Wang recommends a product to a pension fund client. What should he do?
✅ B. Recommend the product most suitable for the client's risk profile

Q2. Analyst Zhao finds his friend holds a stock he is recommending. He needs to:
✅ C. Proactively disclose the conflict of interest

Q3. Which action violates the Code of Ethics?
✅ C. Guaranteeing to a client that "this stock will definitely go up"

📌 Key Takeaways

  • Clients' interests first is the core principle.
  • Insider trading, front running, and guaranteeing returns are explicit violations.
  • Ethics module weight is the highest; requires scenario judgment.
